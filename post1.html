Understanding Machine Learning: A Beginner's Guide

by Gopika on August 16, 2024

Machine learning is a fascinating and rapidly evolving field of artificial intelligence that allows computers to learn from data and make decisions without being explicitly programmed. In this blog post, we will explore the fundamental concepts of machine learning and how they can be applied to various real-world problems.

We will discuss key concepts such as supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, and the types of problems machine learning can solve. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, this guide will help you understand the basics and spark your interest in machine learning.
